Biography

Perparim Çalaj has spent over fifteen years shaping the built environment of Kosovo — one carefully considered project at a time.

Born and raised in Prishtina, Perparim developed a fascination with the relationship between space and human experience from an early age. After completing his architecture degree at the University of Prishtina, he went on to earn a Master's degree, specialising in sustainable urban environments and contemporary materiality.

In 2009, he founded Modul Projekt with a simple but powerful conviction: that architecture should serve people without sacrificing beauty, and celebrate context without becoming nostalgic. The studio quickly earned a reputation for work that is precise, grounded, and quietly ambitious.

Today, Modul Projekt's portfolio spans private residences, cultural institutions, commercial developments, and public infrastructure — each project a study in the dialogue between programme, place, and material.

Philosophy

How We Think

01

Context

Every building is a response to its place — the geography, culture, climate, and stories already present in the site. We listen before we draw.

02

Precision

Details are not decoration — they are the language through which a building speaks its truth. We believe a single well-resolved joint is worth a thousand rendered images.

03

Endurance

We design for decades, not seasons. Buildings should age with dignity, deepen with use, and remain relevant long after the trends that surrounded their birth have faded.
