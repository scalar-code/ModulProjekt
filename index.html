Architecture as
Living Art

Perparim Çalaj is the founder and lead architect of Modul Projekt, a design studio rooted in the belief that architecture should feel inevitable — as though no other form was possible.

With a practice spanning residential, cultural, and commercial work, Perparim brings a meticulous sensibility to every commission. Each project begins with deep listening — to the site, the client, and the silent requirements of light, material, and time.

◈ Master of Architecture — University of Prishtina
◈ Licensed Architect — Kosovo Chamber of Architects
◈ Founder, Modul Projekt Studio

Philosophy
"Architecture is the learned game, correct and magnificent, of forms assembled in the light." — Le Corbusier

Context

Every building is a response to its place — geography, culture, and the human stories that surround it.

Precision

Details are not decoration — they are the language through which a building speaks its truth.

Endurance

We design for decades, not trends — buildings that age with dignity and deepen with time.
