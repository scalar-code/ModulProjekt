Meet Perparim

Perparim Çalaj is the founder and lead architect of Modul Projekt — a studio where precision, context, and enduring beauty converge.

With over 15 years of practice across Kosovo and the wider Balkans, Perparim brings a meticulous sensibility to every commission. His work spans villas, cultural centres, public pavilions, and mixed-use developments, each shaped by a deep commitment to material honesty and spatial quality.
