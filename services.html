Architectural Design

Full-scope architectural services from initial brief through construction completion. We work across residential, commercial, cultural, and mixed-use typologies, bringing the same rigour and attention to a family home as to a public building. Our process begins with deep listening — to the client, the site, and the requirements of the brief — before a single line is drawn.

Interior Architecture

Spatial planning and interior design that extends the architectural language inward. We believe the inside and outside of a building should speak the same language — material choices, proportions, and details that cohere from the facade to the smallest fitting. Our interior work is not decoration; it is architecture continued by other means.

Spatial Planning Material Specification Lighting Design Custom Joinery Furniture Curation
03

Urban Planning

Masterplanning and urban design for mixed-use developments, new neighbourhoods, public realm improvements, and town centre revitalisation. We approach urban design with the same care for human experience as we bring to individual buildings — because cities are nothing more than buildings and the spaces between them, arranged well or poorly.

Masterplanning Public Space Design Zoning Analysis Infrastructure Planning Community Engagement

Consulting & Feasibility

Expert architectural consultation for clients at the earliest stages of a project. We conduct site assessments, programme feasibility studies, and design viability reviews — helping developers, institutions, and private clients understand the full potential and realistic constraints of their vision before committing to a full design commission.

Site Assessment Feasibility Studies Budget Guidance Planning Advice Peer Review
Our Process

How We Work

01
Listen & Discover

Every project begins with listening — to you, to the site, and to the brief. We take time to understand not just what you want, but why, and what the project must truly achieve.

02
Concept & Strategy

We develop a clear design concept that responds to context, budget, and programme. Ideas are tested, refined, and stress-tested before we commit to a direction.

03
Design & Detail

Every element is resolved — from the building's massing to the profile of a window frame. We produce comprehensive technical drawings and coordinate with all specialist consultants.

04
Build & Deliver

We stay closely involved during construction, making site visits, resolving questions, and ensuring the built result faithfully reflects the designed intent. Quality at every step.

FAQ

Common Questions

How long does an architectural project typically take?+
It depends on the scale and complexity. A private villa typically takes 8–14 months from first meeting to construction completion. A larger commercial or cultural building can take 2–4 years. We'll give you a realistic timeline at the very start of your project.
What information do you need to begin a project?+
We need to understand your vision, your budget, and your site. A site survey or property documents are helpful, but an initial conversation is all we need to get started. We'll guide you through everything else.
Do you work outside Kosovo?+
Yes. While most of our work is in Kosovo, we have experience working across the wider Balkans and welcome enquiries from further afield. Each project is assessed on its own merits — geography is rarely an obstacle for the right project.
How do you charge for your services?+
Our fees are calculated as a percentage of the construction cost, which varies by project type, complexity, and the level of service required. We're transparent about fees from the start and provide a clear fee proposal before any work begins.
Can you help with planning permission?+
Absolutely. Navigating the planning system in Kosovo is something we do every day. We prepare all necessary documentation, liaise directly with municipal authorities, and manage the process on your behalf.

Ready to begin?

Tell us about your project and we'll respond within one business day.
